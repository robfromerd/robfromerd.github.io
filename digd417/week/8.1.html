Code Sample 8.1: Pushing Our Sites to WP Engine

These two simple tasks are all you have to do to complete the mid-term quiz, which you should complete by the morning of Saturday October 17th, when I will be submitting the mid-term grades.

Task 1: Pushing to WP Engine

  1. From the WP Engine website for your account, go to the drop down next to your account name in the far upper right and choose "My Profile."
  2. Then at the next screen, you will see a list of small links on the near left where you will choose "API Access".
  3. Click the large purple "Generate Credentials" button in the upper right.
  4. Copy these credentials to a place where you can retrieve them later.
  5. Go back to your Local by Flywheel APPLICATION that resides on your computer now that you are armed with these API credentials.
  6. In the lower right corner of the Local interface, where you will see Connect to: no host selected, " choose WP Engine and then enter the previously copied API credentials.
  7. Then the interface will ask you "Where should we push . . . ?" – choose your site name up on WP Engine.
  8. Then at the next screen, select the "PRODUCTION" environment, AND MAKE SURE you check the box for "include database."
  9. Wait a few minutes for the upload to complete (it should tell you when it has moved from files to the database) and then check that your Remote site now looks like your Local site.
  10. DM or email me the Link to your Remote site
